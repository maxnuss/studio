PlayThru Online Games

by Are You a Human

PlayThru was a series of 10-second online games that served as a replacement for CAPTCHA, the annoying sets of jumbled letters that websites deploy to verify that their users are real humans. The company sold space inside PlayThru to consumer brands as a new form of interactive advertising.
